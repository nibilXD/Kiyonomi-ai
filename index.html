About Us

SNGSHS Karamuck was established in 1920 and is managed by the Pvt. Aided. Located in the rural area of Anthikkad block of Thrissur district, Kerala, it offers education from Grades 1 to 10. Our mission is to provide quality education that helps students develop academically, socially, and emotionally. We have modern facilities like high-tech classrooms, highly skilled teachers, a playground, a library with 2000 books, 51 functional computers, and a mid-day meal program. We offer both English and Malayalam medium education.

Our Services

We provide an enriching curriculum, modern infrastructure, and extracurricular activities to foster growth and development in students. Our facilities include state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, sports facilities, and access to digital resources. We also provide a safe, healthy, and conducive environment for learning and growth.
